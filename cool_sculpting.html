CoolSculpting® non-surgical fat reduction

Sometimes, no matter how hard you diet and exercise, you may still have stubborn areas of fat that just won’t go away. Before you turn to invasive procedures such as liposuction or plastic surgery, consider CoolSculpting®. It’s the most trusted, noninvasive, permanent fat-removal technology available.

CoolSculpting is a nonsurgical, fat-reduction treatment utilizing controlled cooling to eliminate stubborn fat which can be resistant to diet and exercise. CoolSculpting® is the only FDA-cleared cooling technology that targets and kills fat cells with no damage to the surrounding tissues. With more than three million treatments worldwide, it has become the world’s number one non-invasive, fat-reducing procedure. It is important to note that the CoolSculpting procedure is not a weight loss solution for people who are obese.

CoolSculpting is performed in the medical office, with the CoolSculpting device placed on the targeted body part. As the cooling begins, you will feel pressure and intense cold. This soon dissipates. Many people read, watch videos, work on their laptop, or even take a nap during their treatment, which can be completed within an hour. Because CoolSculpting is completely non-invasive, clients can typically return to work and normal activities immediately.

Clients may start to see changes as quickly as three weeks after treatment, and will experience the most dramatic results after two months. The body will continue to flush out fat cells for up to four to six months after treatment. Once the treated fat cells are gone, they're gone for good. Additional sessions may further enhance your results.

Dr. Bukov will develop a customized CoolSculpting treatment plan which addresses your problem areas. Your individualized treatment plan will be tailored to your body, your goals, and your budget.
