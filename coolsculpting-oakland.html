About our CoolSculpting Oakland office

COOLSCULPTING OAKLAND AT NOB HILL MEDICAL & AESTHETICS

CoolSculpting Oakland is the East Bay office of Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics, founded by Dr. Konstantin Bukov in 2012. While Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics offers a wide variety of non-surgical aesthetic procedures and treatments, our Oakland facility specializes solely in CoolSculpting. CoolSculpting is an FDA-cleared method of freezing and eliminating stubborn fat that is resistant to diet and exercise, allowing you to contour your problem spots.

Your results and satisfaction are our top priority at CoolSculpting Oakland. Our team of technicians, led by Dr. Konstantin Bukov, are highly trained and experienced in CoolSculpting. Each has studied the full range of CoolSculpting techniques and tools, learning about the science behind Cryolipolysis and how it can sculpt the human anatomy. Our providers bring considerable skill and knowledge to each consultation, procedure, and recovery process. Dr. Bukov is on call for all Oakland CoolSculpting patients during and after procedures.

Our staff has dedicated themselves to helping you reach your desired short-term and long-term body contouring goals. Whether you are looking into CoolSculpting for a small area, such as under the jawline, or are interested in combining the treatment with more extensive body sculpting techniques, CoolSculpting Oakland at Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics is ready to thoughtfully walk you through your aesthetic journey with personalized attention.

CoolSculpting Oakland provides Concierge-level CoolSculpting. Our East Bay office was opened for your comfort and convenience. We are open 7 days a week from 8am-8pm, by appointment only at 415-440-6800.
