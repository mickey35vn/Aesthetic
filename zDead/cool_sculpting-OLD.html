CoolSculpting® non-surgical fat reduction

You’ve done the work — a clean diet and hours at the gym — and still, that muffin top persists? It doesn’t seem fair given all of your hard work. And it isn’t. But reality is that, in many cases, no amount of diet and exercise will eliminate what Mother Nature and Father Time have conspired to create. For years, the only option patients had to resolve areas of unwanted fat was liposuction. Although liposuction is a very effective solution, it is a semi-invasive procedure, with days of downtime. Now you can address frustrating pockets of fatty tissue with non-surgical Dual CoolSculpting.

CoolSculpting is a highly innovative fat elimination technique, using the science of Cryolipolysis to ‘freeze away’ fat cells. The concentrated cold temperatures break down fat cells, which are safely and naturally eliminated by the body. Improvement can be seen in as little as three weeks, with optimal results in three to four months. A single CoolSculpting session can provide an average of 20-25% reduction in fat tissue.

Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics has multiple CoolSculpting machines. This is the Gold Standard of CoolSculpting services! We are able to reduce your treatment time by utilizing dual machines based upon your treatment plan.

Advantages of CoolSculpting:

  • No incisions
  • No downtime
  • No scarring
  • Efficient — 35-75 minutes per area/cycle
  • One session can achieve an average 20-25% reduction in fat
  • Little chance for side-effects or complications

CoolSculpting can reduce stubborn fatty tissue almost any place on the body. The most commonly treated areas include the abdomen (muffin top), flanks (love handles), inner/outer thighs, distal thigh (top of knee), back fat, upper arms, bra fat and underneath the buttock (banana roll). CoolSculpting's can even help with small, hard to treat areas, such as submental fat (the double chin).


COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT COOLSCULPTING

How does CoolSculpting work?

Fat cells freeze at higher temperatures compared to the surrounding tissues. By using Coolsculpting technology, we can deliver a precise cooling to the targeted area. The fat cells in the area are then crystallized and die. Over time, the body eliminates the dead cells.

How long does CoolSculpting take to see results?

Usually, you'll see the results about three weeks after the procedure but the most dramatic changes are usually apparent two months after the CoolSculpting procedure.

Are CoolSculpting results permanent?

Once the fat cells die and they are being eliminated from the body, they're gone for good. The area that has been already treated will never accumulate as much fat as before.

Where can CoolSculpting be used?

CoolScolpting is currently FDA cleared for the following areas: abdomen, flank (“love handles”), inner thigh, outer thigh and double chin.

CoolSculpting versus liposuction?

CoolSculpting is a nonsurgical procedure, and this is the main advantage and differentiating factor from liposuction. There is no anesthesia, no surgical incisions, no bruising, no post-surgical complications and, most importantly, no downtime after the procedure. Anyone can have it done and return to their normal activities right away.
