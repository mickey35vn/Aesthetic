SkinTyte™

Loose skin, due to weight loss, age, or heredity can be unsightly and affect a person’s self esteem. While plastic surgery can be an option, SkinTyte™ provides effective non-surgical skin tightening. At a fraction of the cost of plastic surgery, SkinTyte™ has become a popular and economic solution to many who suffer from loose skin.

SkinTyte™ utilizes infrared energy to deeply heat soft tissue while protecting the entire treated area with sapphire contact cooling. Targeted heating causes soft tissue coagulation and collagen contraction. This initiates the body’s natural healing process, resulting in a firmer, more youthful skin appearance. The full effect of skin firming treatments can be seen over several subsequent months.

SkinTyte™ can be used on many parts of the body where sagging or loose skin has become an issue, such as the neck, face, stomach, etc. Since there is no downtime, patients may resume their regular activities immediately following treatment.

Dr. Bukov is experienced with the SkinTyte™ system, and will work with clients to achieve the greatest results possible.
