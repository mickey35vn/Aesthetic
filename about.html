About Our office

Great primary care has to do with listening, and by doing so I'm able to offer preventative care options for my patients.

Dr. Konstantin Bukov has offered his patients the latest preventive and anti-aging medicine at his office in the heart of San Francisco since 2013.

He opened his practice in Lower Pacific Heights with zero patients and grew it by focusing on the patient experience and delivering excellent quality care. In early 2016, he moved into a larger, more comfortable office to continue delivering the best care possible.

Today, his patients follow him and refer their friends for his care because of his compassionate nature, friendly sense of humor, and attention to their needs.

More than primary care, Dr. Bukov enjoys providing aesthetic medical services to help patients feel and look their best. Witnessing the difference in confidence of his patients who receive treatments like Coolsculpting, Botox, fillers, and laser treatments is one of the best parts of his day. He loves that he can help healthy people feel even better through the aesthetic side of his practice.

Dr. Bukov is committed to investing in the latest technologies so he can continue providing the latest and greatest treatments for his patients. All of the lasers and tools he uses in his office are hand made in Palo Alto by Sciton, the maker of the world’s best medical aesthetic lasers. For injectables, he only uses the highest quality FDA-approved products, and he only purchases them through US manufacturers—with no unvetted middlemen or diluted product offerings.

Dr. Bukov is unique in his field for the unparalleled level of accessibility he provides to his patients. Not only can patients instantly book appointments online, but he gives his personal cell phone number to patients so they can contact him at any time with questions about their care or in case of an emergency.

Dr. Bukov is board certified by the American Board of Family Medicine.


Personal Interests

In his free time, Dr. Bukov enjoys playing tennis and taking ski trips when he gets the chance. Ouside of the office, he can be found soaking up the beautiful Bay Area weather.
