Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics

Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetics is the premier medical wellness and skin care laser center, located in the heart of San Francisco’s Nob Hill district. We specialize in an holistic approach, non-surgical, non-invasive procedures designed to slow down the aging process via state-of -the art technology.

Under Dr. Konstantin Bukov's leadership, we are one of the first few offices in the bay area to bring San Francisco the latest anti-aging technologically advanced products such as the breakthrough innovation of Forever Young BBL™(broad Band Light), laser skin care therapy, for use on face and body, and diVa™, the vaginal rejuvenation laser therapy. These breakthrough technologies and latest Innovation are created and manufactured by Sciton, based in Palo Alto. These newest equipment and therapies Demonstrate Our commitment to bring you only the very best technologies locally and globally.
