O-Shot®

How many times have you “seen the finish line”, but were simply unable to cross it? Up to 25% of women have an orgasmic dysfunction, which is defined as the persistent or recurrent delay or absence of orgasm following a normal sexual excitement phase. The list of reasons this might happen is long and includes medications, hormone issues, medical problems or relationship issues. Research supports that better sex is more fun; better sex also enhances creativity, relieves stress, and improves self-confidence and overall health.

The O-Shot® is an all-natural procedure utilizing your own blood platelets to rejuvenate and revitalize vaginal and clitoral function, giving you significant improvement with sexual desire and pleasure, improving urinary incontinence and vaginal dryness.

This in-office procedure begins with a blood draw. Then, using a proprietary technique, the growth factors are extracted from your blood and concentrated into the Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP). The PRP is then injected into numbed areas for a painless treatment experience. The O-Shot® has virtually no downtime. Results appear 4 weeks after treatment and last up to 18 months.

Dr. Bukov will thoroughly explain the procedure and allay any fears you may have during your initial consultation.
