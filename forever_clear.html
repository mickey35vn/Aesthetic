Forever Clear Broad Band Light (BBL™)

* Individual result may vary


Acne and the skin damage it leaves behind can start during puberty or well into adulthood. The emotional damage associated with acne lesions and scarring are difficult to handle no matter what your age.

Have you “tried everything” to clear up your problem complexion? Hydrogen peroxide, tea tree oil, topical gels, Proactive, Obagi, antibiotics, and Accutane can be effective solutions, until they aren’t. At some point you may find your skin has developed resistance to your acne therapy, and change is needed.

Forever Clear Broad Band Light (BBL™) has been proven effective in the treatment of acne. Sciton BBL™ is an FDA-approved laser acne treatment that uses a blue BBL light to target the p-acnes bacteria deep within the pores that are partly to blame for acne. Once these bacteria are eliminated, skin is then treated with red BBL light to reduce the inflammation and acne-associated redness to give you healthy, luminous skin.

During your treatment, Dr. Bukov will apply a thin layer of gel to your skin, followed by the BBL™ Laser Acne Treatment. Forever Clear is great for both teens and adults, because it is non-invasive, fast, virtually painless, and typically allows patients to wear make-up right after and go back to their normal activities immediately.

To achieve optimal results with the BBL™ Laser Acne Treatment, a series of 8 weekly treatments is suggested. You may need a periodic touch-up treatment every month to every 6 months, depending on your acne type and severity.

Along with a home skincare program designed for acne-prone skin, you can enjoy clear skin. Schedule your consultation with Nob Hill Medical & Aesthetic today.
